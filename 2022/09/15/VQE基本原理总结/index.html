将 HHH 映射为

H=∑kckHkHk=Z0⊗X1⊗Y2⊗⋯⊗ZN−1H=\sum_k c_kH_k\\ H_k=Z_0\otimes X_1\otimes Y_2\otimes\cdots\otimes Z_{N-1} H=k∑​ck​Hk​Hk​=Z0​⊗X1​⊗Y2​⊗⋯⊗ZN−1​

H′=H+α∣E0⟩⟨E0∣H^\prime= H+\alpha|E_0\rangle\langle E_0| H′=H+α∣E0​⟩⟨E0​∣
