0-1背包

发表于2020-05-05|更新于2023-01-31|算法
|字数总计:2.5k|阅读时长:12分钟|阅读量:

定义

有 NNN 件物品和一个容量为 VVV 的背包。放入第 iii 件物品消耗的体积为 CiC_iCi​，得到的价值是 WiW_iWi​ 。求解在背包容量允许的情况下，能获得的最大价值；即选取 $i_1,i_2,\cdots,i_k$，求 $\max\limits_{\sum\limits_{j=1}^kC_{i_j}\le V}\sum\limits_{j=1}^kW_{i_j}$ 。

基本思路-动态规划 (Dynamic Programming)

暴力枚举

这个问题的特点是，每种物品都只有一件，可以选择放或者不放；即放入背包为 111 ，否则为 000 ，故称 0−10-10−1 背包。按照状态压缩枚举的思想，共有 2N2^N2N 种选取形式，时间复杂度为 O(2N)O(2^N)O(2N) ，时间复杂度是不可接受的。事实上可以利用动态规划的思想，记录有效信息，降低时间复杂度。

状态转移方程

定义 dp[i][j]dp[i][j]dp[i][j] 为在前 iii 个物品中挑选总容量不超过 jjj 的物品时，能够获得的最大价值。
有状态转移方程

dp[i][j]={dp[i−1][j]j<Cimax⁡(dp[i−1][j−Ci]+Wi,dp[i−1][j])j≥Cidp[i][j]=\begin{cases}dp[i-1][j]&j<C_i\\\max(dp[i-1][j-C_i]+W_i,dp[i-1][j])&j\ge C_i\end{cases} dp[i][j]={dp[i−1][j]max(dp[i−1][j−Ci​]+Wi​,dp[i−1][j])​j<Ci​j≥Ci​​

每次状态转移时都需要决策，是否将第 iii 件物品放入背包。如果不装入第 iii 件物品，那么预留给前 $ i-1$ 个物品的容量仍然为 jjj ，可由 dp[i−1][j]dp[i-1][j]dp[i−1][j] 转移；如果将第 iii 件物品放入背包，由于给前 iii 个物品的容量最多为 jjj ，那么预留给前 i−1i-1i−1 件物品的容量只剩下 j−Cij-C_ij−Ci​，剩下的 CiC_iCi​ 用来装第 iii 个物品，因此前一个状态是 dp[i−1][j−Ci]dp[i-1][j-C_i]dp[i−1][j−Ci​] 。当 j<Cij<C_ij<Ci​ ，背包显然装不下，因此 dp[i][j]=dp[i−1][j]dp[i][j]=dp[i-1][j]dp[i][j]=dp[i−1][j]；当 j≥Cij\ge C_ij≥Ci​ ，需要考虑是否将第 iii 件物品放入背包，因此 dp[i][j]=max⁡(dp[i−1][j−Ci]+Wi,dp[i−1][j])dp[i][j]=\max(dp[i-1][j-C_i]+W_i,dp[i-1][j])dp[i][j]=max(dp[i−1][j−Ci​]+Wi​,dp[i−1][j])。

核心代码

根据定义，dp[i][j]dp[i][j]dp[i][j] 是 dp[N][V]dp[N][V]dp[N][V] （在前 iii 个物品中挑选总容量不超过 jjj 的物品时，能够获得的最大价值）的子问题，当得到 dp[i][j]dp[i][j]dp[i][j] 时，可通过转移方程最终得到 dp[N][V]dp[N][V]dp[N][V] ；当得到 dp[N][V]dp[N][V]dp[N][V] 后，∀ i≤N,j≤V\forall\ i\le N,j\le V∀ i≤N,j≤V，dp[i][j]dp[i][j]dp[i][j] 都已确定；因此该问题满足最优子结构。当计算 dp[i][j]dp[i][j]dp[i][j] 时，dp[i−1][j]dp[i-1][j]dp[i−1][j] 和 dp[i−1][j−Ci]dp[i-1][j-C_i]dp[i−1][j−Ci​] 都已经被更新，因此该问题含有若干重叠子问题。当 dp[i][j]dp[i][j]dp[i][j] 已知，只需要通过少数之前的状态转移到下一个状态，不必在意所有的转移路径，因此该问题具有无后效性。

HDU 2602 Bone Collector ↬\looparrowright↬

Problem Description

Many years ago , in Teddy’s hometown there was a man who was called “Bone Collector”. This man like to collect varies of bones , such as dog’s , cow’s , also he went to the grave …
The bone collector had a big bag with a volume of VVV ,and along his trip of collecting there are a lot of bones , obviously , different bone has different value and different volume, now given the each bone’s value along his trip , can you calculate out the maximum of the total value the bone collector can get ?

Input

The first line contain a integer TTT , the number of cases.
Followed by TTT cases , each case three lines , the first line contain two integer N,V,(N≤1000,V≤1000)N , V, (N \le 1000 , V \le 1000 )N,V,(N≤1000,V≤1000) representing the number of bones and the volume of his bag. And the second line contain NNN integers representing the value of each bone. The third line contain NNN integers representing the volume of each bone.

Output

One integer per line representing the maximum of the total value (this number will be less than 231231231).

Sample Input

1
5 10
1 2 3 4 5
5 4 3 2 1

Sample Output

14

Translation

有一个喜欢收集各种骨头的人，他有一个容积为 vvv 的背包。现在给出 nnn 个骨头的体积与价值，在背包容量允许的情况下，求能够收集到的骨头的最大总价值。

Idea

0−10-10−1 背包模型。定义 dp[i][j]dp[i][j]dp[i][j] 为在前 iii 个物品中挑选总容量不超过 jjj 的物品时，能够获得的最大价值。
有状态转移方程

dp[i][j]={dp[i−1][j]j<volume[i]max⁡(dp[i−1][j−volume[i]]+value[i],dp[i−1][j])j≥volume[i]dp[i][j]=\begin{cases}dp[i-1][j]&j<volume[i]\\\max(dp[i-1][j-volume[i]]+value[i],dp[i-1][j])&j\ge volume[i]\end{cases} dp[i][j]={dp[i−1][j]max(dp[i−1][j−volume[i]]+value[i],dp[i−1][j])​j<volume[i]j≥volume[i]​

时间复杂度为 O(nv)O(nv)O(nv) ，空间复杂度为 O(nv)O(nv)O(nv) 。

POJ 3624 Charm Bracelet ↬\looparrowright↬

Description

Bessie has gone to the mall’s jewelry store and spies a charm bracelet. Of course, she’d like to fill it with the best charms possible from the NNN(1≤N≤3402)(1 ≤ N ≤ 3402)(1≤N≤3402) available charms. Each charm iii in the supplied list has a weight WiW_iWi​(1≤Wi≤400)(1 ≤ W_i ≤ 400)(1≤Wi​≤400), a desirability factor DiD_iDi​(1≤Di≤100)(1 ≤ D_i ≤ 100)(1≤Di​≤100), and can be used at most once. Bessie can only support a charm bracelet whose weight is no more than M(1≤M≤12880)M(1 ≤ M ≤ 12880)M(1≤M≤12880).
Given that weight limit as a constraint and a list of the charms with their weights and desirability rating, deduce the maximum possible sum of ratings.

Input

Line 111: Two space-separated integers, NNN and MMM.
Line 222 to N+1N+1N+1: Line i+1i+1i+1 describes charm iii with two space-separated integers, WiW_iWi​ and DiD_iDi​.

Output

Line 1: A single integer that is the greatest sum of charm desirabilities that can be achieved given the weight constraints.

Sample Input

4 6
1 4
2 6
3 12
2 7

Sample Output

23

Translation

一个人去珠宝店偷东西，他最多只能拿得到总重量为 MMM 的珠宝。现在给出 NNN 件物品的质量和价值，在允许的重量范围内，求能够收集到的珠宝的最大总价值。

Idea

0−10-10−1 背包模型，由于数据较大，需要进行空间优化。
定义 dp[j]dp[j]dp[j] 表示背包容量为 jjj 时能够获得的最大价值。

时间复杂度为 O(NM)O(NM)O(NM) ，空间复杂度为 O(M)O(M)O(M) 。
