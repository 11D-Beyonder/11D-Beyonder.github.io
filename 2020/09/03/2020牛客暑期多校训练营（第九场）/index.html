A. Groundhog and 2-Power Representation ↬\looparrowright↬

题目描述

Groundhog took a math class. In this class, his math teacher said, any positive integer can be represented by the power of 222. For example, 137=27+23+20137=2^7+2^3+2^0137=27+23+20.
And powers are expressed in parentheses. That is, a(b)a(b)a(b) stands for aba^bab. Therefore, 137137137 can be expressed as 137=2(7)+2(3)+2(0)137=2(7)+2(3)+2(0)137=2(7)+2(3)+2(0).
Further more,for 7=22+2+207=2^2+2+2^07=22+2+20 is expressed with 222, 3=2+203=2+2^03=2+20,137 can be finally expressed as 137=2(2(2)+2+2(0))+2(2+2(0))+2(0)137=2(2(2)+2+2(0))+2(2+2(0))+2(0)137=2(2(2)+2+2(0))+2(2+2(0))+2(0).
Another example, 1315=210+28+25+2+1=1315=2^{10}+2^8+2^5+2+1 =1315=210+28+25+2+1=$ 2(2(2+2(0))+2)+2(2(2+2(0)))+2(2(2)+2(0))+2+2(0)$.
Groundhog feels amazing and wants you to write a program to simulate the above content. You need to read in an expression that is a power of 222 and calculate its value.

I. Groundhog Looking Dowdy ↬\looparrowright↬

题目描述

Today, ZLZX has a mysterious case, Orange lost his down jacket hanging in his dorm room. Under the expectations of everyone, detective Groundhog took his small spoon of the artifact and started the journey to solve the case.
After an in-depth investigation of the northernmost mysterious room on each floor, Groundhog discovered nnn mysterious numbers. As long as the clues conveyed by these numbers are deciphered, he can reveal the truth of the matter. The deciphering method is, using these numbers to generate two positive integers without leading zeros, and minimizing the product of these two positive integers is the final clue.
Then Groundhog wants to know what the smallest product is?
As he continued to investigate in the room west of the new building, he gave you the question.
Concise meaning, given nnn numbers between 000 and 999, use them to make two positive integers without leading zeros to minimize the product.

输入描述

The first line of input is a single integer TTT, the number of test cases.
For each set of data, each test case begins with a single integer nnn , the count of numbers.
The next line are nnn numbers.

输出描述

For each set of case, an integer is output, representing the smallest product.

There are at least two Numbers that are guaranteed not to be zero.
The Numbers range between [0,9][0,9][0,9].

分析

若不考虑前导零的限制，取 xxx 为 nnn 个数中的最小值，yyy 为其余 n−1n-1n−1 个数组合起来的最小整数，x×yx\times yx×y 即为最小乘积。
考虑前导零后，xxx 为 nnn 个数中的最小正整数，yyy 为其余 n−1n-1n−1 个数组合起来的最小正整数。具体方法为：将 nnn 个数从小到大排序，取最小的正整数为 xxx；在剩余 n−1n-1n−1 个数中，取最小正整数为 yyy 的最高位，然后向 yyy 的低位补零，最后向 yyy 的低位从小到大不正整数。最后模拟个位数乘多位数的乘法输出 x×yx\times yx×y。
